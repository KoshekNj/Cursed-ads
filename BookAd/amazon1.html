    About

    The Amazon’s bestselling author, internet famous book reviewer and The Hutchinson Church Book Club leader Samantha M. Johnson is back with another dark fantasy erotica that will haunt your imagination for days.

    It was just another day of mushrooms foraging for Demiana, a lovely wife of the hardworking royal wood chopper from the lush region of Lignuma, when suddenly a wolf appears before her. He is not agressive, his eyes are warm and inviting, giving her a sudden urge to get close to him, to follow him...and so she does, leading her to a long abandoned castle, in which nothing is as it seems, especially not the wolf. As home life gets shakier and visits to the castle get more sensual, Demiana knows her life will never be the same. The question is, how high of the price is she willing to pay for revival of her long lost desire?
