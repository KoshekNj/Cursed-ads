About

Spaecraft is a free-to-play MMORPG set in Matrihsia, the distant galaxy densely inhabited by several wild, murderous, and power-hungry tribes of women. The Queen is scrambling to form an army that will bring peace to the galaxy, but emotions are high and keeping her warriors loyal gets harder as each day full of rumors and love games passes in the chaos of wartime.

  • Choose between 7 mouth-watering, forget-you-have-a-wife-at-home kind of sexy female warriors
  • Wield swords, num-chuks, shoot lasers, deflect bullets with your humongous chest – the opportunities are vast
  • Play your cards right and you might find yourself in some really steamy situations

Join our badass space ladies in this epic journey full of strategy, action, plot twists, spaceships and of course...personalities ;)
